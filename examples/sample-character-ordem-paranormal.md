# Sample Character: Ordem Paranormal

> Example output generated by `rpg-lore-weaver` for an Ordem Paranormal campaign with an investigative horror tone.

```


════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  LUCAS FERREIRA — The One Who Listened
════════════════════════════════════════════════════

 📌 ELEVATOR PITCH
   A former university radio host recruited by the
   Ordo Realitas after broadcasting a frequency that
   shouldn't exist  now he hears whispers from
   O Outro Lado every time he puts on headphones.

 👤 APPEARANCE & VIBE
   Lean, mid-twenties, perpetual dark circles under
   bloodshot eyes. Wears a faded university hoodie
   and always carries a pair of vintage over-ear
   headphones around his neck  never wireless,
   never Bluetooth. Fidgets with the cord when
   nervous. Speaks quickly, interrupts himself,
   then apologizes. Smells faintly of cheap coffee
   and soldering flux. Has a nervous laugh that
   comes out at exactly the wrong moments. Left
   earlobe slightly scarred from where he clawed
   at it during his first episode.

 📖 BACKSTORY
   Lucas ran the midnight slot at Rádio UFMG in
   Belo Horizonte  three hours of ambient music
   and philosophy for insomniacs and lonely hearts.
   One Thursday at 2:47 AM, while adjusting his
   equipment, he caught a signal on a frequency
   that shouldn't exist: 108.7 FM, past the legal
   broadcast spectrum. Static at first, then a
   voice. Not words  more like the shape of words.
   The feeling of being spoken to without language.

   He recorded it. Played it back. The recording
   was silent. But his sound equipment registered
   waveforms  complex, structured, intentional
   patterns that no known source could produce.

   He became obsessed. Spent three weeks modifying
   his equipment, sleeping at the station, skipping
   classes. His roommate filed a missing persons
   report. His mother called seventeen times. He
   answered none of them because the signal was
   getting clearer.

   On the fourth week, the signal spoke his name.
   Not his on-air name. His real name. The one
   only his dead grandmother used: "Lucão."

   He screamed. Tore off his headphones. Ran out
   of the station at 3 AM and sat on the sidewalk
   until morning. A woman in a dark suit found him
   there, still shaking, still hearing the echo.
   She said: "We know what you heard. We can help
   you understand it  or we can make you forget."

   Lucas chose to understand. He hasn't stopped
   regretting it since.

 🧠 PSYCHOLOGICAL PROFILE
  • Personality: Hypercurious to the point of
    self-destruction. Talks too much when nervous
    (which is always). Genuinely kind, but kindness
    gets buried under paranoia after missions.
    Copes with humor  bad humor. The kind that
    makes everyone uncomfortable at debriefs.
  • Ideals: Knowledge should never be hidden, even
    when it's dangerous. People deserve to know
    what's out there. (He's starting to doubt this.)
  • Bonds: His mother in BH who thinks he got a
    government job in Brasília. His old roommate
    Rafael, who still texts him memes at 2 AM
    without knowing anything. The headphones
    they were his grandmother's, and he can't
    bring himself to throw them away.
  • Flaws: Cannot resist investigating a signal,
    a sound, a whisper  even when every training
    protocol says to stop. Lies compulsively about
    how he's doing. Says "I'm fine" like it's
    a reflex, even when his hands are shaking.
  • Fear: That the voice on 108.7 knows him better
    than he knows himself. That it's still
    broadcasting. That one day he'll tune in again
     not because he has to, but because he
    wants to.
  • Desire vs Need: WANTS to decode the signal and
    understand what spoke to him  to prove it was
    real, to prove he's not crazy. NEEDS to accept
    that some things speak not to be understood, but
    to be invited in  and that his obsession is
    the door.

  NEX: 15%  Low but climbing. Occasional auditory
  distortions. Hears faint static in silence.
  Sanidade: Stable on the surface. Fragile underneath.

 🔗 CONNECTIONS
  • Ally: Agent Camila Duarte  Field partner, former
    military police. Doesn't understand his tech
    babble but trusts his instincts. She's the one
    who physically pulls him away from equipment
    when he zones out. He's never told her about
    the voice saying his grandmother's name.
  • Mentor: Dr. Helena Voss  Senior Ordo researcher
    who recruited him. Cold, precise, never wastes
    words. Taught him that "curiosity without
    discipline is just another name for self-harm."
    Lucas suspects she's heard the frequency too.
    She refuses to discuss it.
  • Rival: Agent Pedro Menezes  Another recruit who
    joined the same month. Specializes in Elemento
    de Conhecimento. Pedro thinks Lucas is reckless
    and will get the team killed. Lucas thinks Pedro
    is a coward who uses protocol as an excuse to
    never take risks. They're both right.

 🎣 DM HOOKS
  • Secret: Lucas still has the modified radio
    equipment in a storage unit in BH. He pays
    for it monthly. He hasn't gone back. But he
    hasn't canceled the rental either.
  • Unresolved Thread: Three other university radio
    stations across Brazil reported "anomalous
    frequency events" in the same week Lucas heard
    108.7 FM. The other hosts are missing. The Ordo
    classified the case. Lucas found the file number
    but hasn't had the courage to open it.
  • Evolution Arc: Lucas must learn the difference
    between seeking answers and seeking the feeling
    of being chosen. The frequency didn't pick him
    because he's special  it picked him because
    he was lonely enough to listen.
  • Cultural Touchstone: During the Festa de São João
    in BH, the old community radio tradition keeps
    Lucas tied to places, voices, and unresolved memory.


```
