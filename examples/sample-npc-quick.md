# Sample NPC: Quick Mode

> Example output generated using the NPC Quick Mode (`references/npc-quick-mode.md`).
> Shows all three tiers of NPC depth for the same character.

---

## Tier 1: Background NPC

**Rapid-Fire Input**: Blacksmith, Nervous, Owes money

```
 🔨 UNNAMED BLACKSMITH
   Hook: Flinches at loud noises and apologizes for it.
   Want: Needs coin to pay off a debt before the end of the month.
   Crack: Will overcharge or cut corners if desperate enough.
```

_Total creation time: ~30 seconds_

---

## Tier 2: Functional NPC (Expanded)

**Expanding the same NPC for a more involved scene:**

```

   🔨 DORIN HALFHAMMER — The Flinching Smith


   ONE-LINE HOOK
     A blacksmith whose hands are steady on the
     anvil but shake everywhere else.

   WANT
     600 gold pieces before the new moon. He owes
     it to a merchant guild that financed his shop.
     Miss the deadline and he loses everything
     the forge, the house above it, the life he
     built after leaving the army.

   CRACK
     Dorin served in a border war five years ago.
     Saw things. Did things. Came home and opened
     a forge because the rhythm of hammering
     drowns out the memories. But the debt is
     making the memories louder. He's started
     drinking again. His work is slipping. Two
     customers complained last week.

   DM NOTES
     • Will offer the party a 20% discount on
       gear if they help with a "small problem"
     • The merchant guild is connected to [insert
       campaign faction]
     • If pushed too hard, he'll reveal he knows
       how to forge something unusual  a skill
       from his army days he tries to forget

```

_Total creation time: ~2-3 minutes_

## Tier 3: Recurring NPC (Full)

**Expanding further for a character who'll appear across multiple sessions:**

```

   🔨 DORIN HALFHAMMER — The Flinching Smith


   ONE-LINE HOOK
     A blacksmith whose hands are steady on the
     anvil but shake everywhere else.

   WANT
     600 gold pieces before the new moon. He owes
     it to a merchant guild that financed his shop.
     Miss the deadline and he loses everything.

   CRACK
     Dorin served in a border war five years ago.
     The army taught him to forge weapons that
     could pierce magical barriers  a technique
     that's technically classified. He deserted
     when ordered to forge them for a purpose he
     couldn't stomach. The forge, the small-town
     life, the debt  it's all a cover. A life
     built on top of a secret.

   VOICE & MANNERISMS
     Speaks in short sentences. Stutters on words
     starting with 'B' when nervous (which is
     often). Wipes his hands on his apron even
     when they're clean. Laughs too loud at jokes
     that aren't funny. Makes excellent tea and
     always offers it  hospitality is his armor.

    CONNECTIONS
     • WIFE: Marta  Knows about the war. Doesn't
       know about the classified forging technique.
       Thinks the nightmares are just standard war
       trauma. She's the reason he stays sober
       most days.
     • CREDITOR: Guildmaster Serrano  Patient but
       calculating. Doesn't need Dorin's money
       needs Dorin's compliance. Suspects Dorin has
       a valuable military skill.
     • OLD FRIEND: Sergeant Kell  Still serves in
       the army. Sends letters that Dorin doesn't
       open. The last one was marked "URGENT."

    DM HOOKS
     • Serrano doesn't want the money  he wants
       Dorin to forge barrier-piercing weapons for
       a private client. The debt is leverage.
     • Sergeant Kell's urgent letter: the army
       knows Dorin deserted. They're not looking
       for a deserter  they're looking for the
       only person alive who knows the technique.
     • If the party helps Dorin, he can forge them
       ONE exceptional item  but doing so risks
       revealing his location to the army.
     • Marta is pregnant. She hasn't told Dorin.
       The stakes of losing the forge just doubled.

   EVOLUTION POTENTIAL
     SESSION 1: Nervous shopkeeper, comic relief
     SESSION 3: Party discovers the debt is a trap
     SESSION 5: The army arrives. Dorin must choose:
                run again, or stand and face it.
     SESSION 7+: Potential full ally, with a unique
                 crafting ability and a target on
                 his back.


```

_Total creation time: ~5-8 minutes_

## Key Takeaways

This example demonstrates:

1. **Tier escalation** The same NPC grows in depth without contradicting earlier tiers
2. **The 3 pillars** (Hook, Want, Crack) are enough for any tier; higher tiers just add detail
3. **DM Hooks emerge naturally** from the Crack Dorin's secret generates multiple adventure threads
4. **Evolution potential** turns a one-scene NPC into a campaign-spanning character
