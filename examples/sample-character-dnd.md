# Sample Character: D&D 5e

> Example output generated by `rpg-lore-weaver` for a D&D 5e campaign in the Forgotten Realms.

```


════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  KAEL THORNWOOD — The Reluctant Flame
════════════════════════════════════════════════════

 📌 ELEVATOR PITCH
   A tiefling druid who was raised by wood elves and hides
   his infernal heritage beneath a mask of bark and leaves,
   seeking to prove that blood does not define destiny.

 👤 APPEARANCE & VIBE
   Lean, wiry frame with bark-brown skin that almost hides
   the faint crimson undertone. Small horns filed down and
   covered by a crown of woven ivy. Amber eyes that glow
   faintly in darkness. Speaks softly, deliberately, as if
   each word were a seed being planted. Smells of moss and
   rain. Fidgets with a wooden ring when nervous.

 📖 BACKSTORY
   Kael was abandoned as an infant at the edge of the
   Chondalwood  a squalling tiefling left in a basket with
   nothing but a silver locket containing a portrait of a
   human woman with sad eyes. The wood elf druid circle of
   Silvanus found him and, after heated debate, chose to
   raise him as one of their own.

   Elder Sylara became his mother in all but blood. She
   taught him that "the forest does not judge by root, only
   by fruit." He grew up speaking Elvish before Common,
   learning to read weather in the clouds and emotion in
   animal cries. But no amount of ivy could fully hide the
   horns, and the other elf children never let him forget
   what he was.

   At 17, a traveling warlock recognized Kael's bloodline
   and told him his birth mother had made a pact with
   Mephistopheles. Kael has carried this knowledge like a
   stone in his chest ever since  the fear that the fire
   in his blood is not metaphorical.

   His key crossroad came when the warlock offered to teach
   him to embrace his infernal power. Kael refused, chose
   to strengthen his druidic bonds instead, and burned the
   warlock's letter of introduction. But some nights, when
   the campfire flickers, he sees shapes in the flames that
   seem to whisper his true name.

 🧠 PSYCHOLOGICAL PROFILE
  • Personality: Gentle, observant, self-doubting. Laughs
    quietly and rarely. Finds peace in tending growing
    things. Becomes rigid and withdrawn when confronted
    about his heritage.
  • Ideals: Nature is the great equalizer  it cares not
    for bloodlines, only balance. Every creature deserves
    the chance to prove what they choose to be.
  • Bonds: Elder Sylara (adoptive mother), the Chondalwood
    circle, and the silver locket he's never opened again.
  • Flaws: Paralyzing self-doubt. Would rather flee than
    risk losing control. Hides his tiefling features
    compulsively  to the point of deception.
  • Fear: That the warlock was right. That his blood will
    eventually consume the druid within.
  • Desire vs Need: WANTS to be accepted as fully elf/druid.
    NEEDS to accept all parts of himself  including the
    infernal flame.

 🔗 CONNECTIONS
  • Ally: Lirael  A half-elf ranger who discovered Kael's
    secret and kept it. She sends letters via hawk.
  • Mentor: Elder Sylara  Taught him "the forest does not
    judge by root, only by fruit." He clings to this when
    the doubt gets loud.
  • Rival: Thandril  A purist elf druid who believes
    Kael's presence corrupts the circle. Their conflict is
    ideological but personal  Thandril was once his friend.

 🎣 DM HOOKS
  • Secret: Kael's birth mother is alive and searching for
    him  not out of love, but because breaking her pact
    requires the blood of her firstborn.
  • Unresolved Thread: The silver locket contains a map on
    its interior lid, visible only under moonlight. Kael
    has never looked.
  • Evolution Arc: Kael must learn that rejecting his
    infernal side IS the infernal side winning  true
    balance requires integration, not denial.
  • Cultural Touchstone: The druids of Chondalwood still
    recite Elder Sylara's oath at the equinox, tying Kael's
    legacy to a living ritual in the setting.
```
