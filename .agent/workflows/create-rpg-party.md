Follow these steps to create a party of interconnected characters using RPG Lore Weaver.

1. **Load the skill**
   - Read `rpg-lore-weaver/SKILL.md` and `rpg-lore-weaver/references/party-creation-mode.md`.

2. **Party Discovery**
   - Ask: How many characters? System & setting? Tone? Are all PCs or a mix of PCs and NPCs?
   - Ask: Any pre-existing concepts or constraints?

3. **Shared History**
   - Before creating individuals, establish what connects the party:
     - Shared event, location, or faction
     - Pre-existing relationships (at least 2 characters must have history)

4. **Individual Creation (Abbreviated)**
   - For each character, run a compressed version of the 6-phase process.
   - Focus on elements that interconnect with other party members.
   - Cross-reference: each character's connections should reference at least one other party member.

5. **Web of Relationships**
   - Map party dynamics: trust, tension, secrets, and shared goals.
   - Ensure at least one asymmetric relationship (A trusts B, but B has doubts).

6. **Party Document**
   - Generate individual character documents.
   - Add a "Party Dynamics" summary section showing the relationship web.
