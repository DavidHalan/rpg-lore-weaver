# Tormenta 20 Rules Summary (Backstory Focus)

## Key Concepts

### A Tormenta (The Storm)

- **Implication**: A red demon-storm of alien origin that corrupts reality. It is the world's constant existential threat.
- **Backstory**: Everyone in Arton has lost something to the storm or knows someone who has. This is inescapable context.

### Raça (Race)

- **Humanos**: Versatile, most common, diverse cultures across Arton.
- **Elfos**: Long-lived, magical, with a troubled history (many lost their homeland).
- **Anões**: Craftsmen, stubborn, deeply tied to honor and grudges.
- **Lefou**: Corrupted by the storm, outcasts — a living reminder of what the Tormenta takes.
- **Minotauros**: Logical, imposing, from a culture that values strength and reason.
- **Qareen**: Descendants of genies, generous, naturally magical.
- **Goblin**: Small, resourceful, often underestimated — which they use to their advantage.
- **Golem**: Artificial beings seeking purpose and identity.
- **Hynne**: Small and quick, from cheerful agrarian communities.
- **Kliren**: Gnome-like inventors, obsessively curious.
- **Medusa**: Serpentine lower body, often mistrusted, struggling for acceptance.
- **Ostsjöra**: Aquatic race from ocean depths, unfamiliar with surface customs.
- **Sereia/Tritão**: Amphibious, tied to ocean cultures and mysteries.
- **Trog**: Reptilian, tribal, proud warriors.
- **Aggelus / Sulfure**: Celestial / Infernal descendents, walking between worlds.

### Devoção (Devotion to a God)

- **Mechanic**: Following one of the 20 Divindades grants powers (Poderes Concedidos).
- **Narrative**: Gods in Arton are **extremely active**. Losing faith = losing powers. Some gods are dead (like Glórienn), others are contested.
- **Key question**: "Does your character worship a god? If so, WHY — out of gratitude, fear, duty, or desperation?"

### Origem (Origin / Background)

Origem provides skills, proficiencies, and a narrative hook. Examples:

- **Amigo dos Animais**: Raised among creatures, trusts beasts more than people
- **Amnésico**: No memory of the past — the backstory IS the mystery
- **Capanga**: Former thug or enforcer — violence was the job
- **Circense**: Performer, acrobat — the show is the mask
- **Criminoso**: Life of crime, contacts in the underworld
- **Eremita**: Isolated by choice or force, knows a secret
- **Gladiador**: Fought for entertainment — freedom was earned, not given
- **Herói Camponês**: Ordinary person who did something extraordinary once
- **Marujo**: Life at sea, rough edges, superstitious
- **Nobre**: Born into privilege, either embracing or rebelling against it
- **Soldado**: Military training, discipline, PTSD, loyalty to a cause

## Backstory Prompts

### By Class

**Combatentes (Fighter, Cavaleiro, Bárbaro):**

- Who first put a weapon in your hands, and what did they expect in return?
- What battle changed you — not the victory, but the cost?
- What code guides you, and when have you broken it?

**Conjuradores (Arcanista, Bruxo, Mago):**

- How did magic enter your life — by study, blood, or accident?
- What was the first spell that frightened you?
- Who in Arton fears your power, and are they right to?

**Devotos (Clérigo, Druida, Paladino):**

- What moment crystallized your faith? Was it beautiful or terrible?
- Has your Divindade ever been silent when you needed them most?
- What would make you abandon your Devoção?

**Especialistas (Caçador, Ladino, Inventor, Bardo):**

- Where did you learn your craft, and who was your first teacher?
- What job went wrong and taught you the most?
- What's the most valuable thing you've ever stolen, created, or discovered?

### Universal Tormenta 20 Questions

- Have you or someone you love been touched by the Tormenta?
- Where were you during the last surge? What did you lose?
- The Purists (human supremacists) are rising — where do you stand?
- If the Tormenta could be destroyed but it required sacrificing one race, would you do it?

## Connections Prompts

- Who from your Origem still contacts you, and what do they want?
- What rival shares your Devoção but walks a different path?
- Who saved your life and never let you forget it?
- What person from outside Arton do you trust more than any Artoniano?

## Mechanical Suggestions (Phase 6)

### Origem Selection

Choose the Origem that best fits the backstory — not by optimization, but by story truth. A "Herói Camponês" who was actually a farmer is more compelling than an optimized attribute spread.

### Atributos

Derive from backstory: Força, Destreza, Constituição, Inteligência, Sabedoria, Carisma. Suggest based on life experience.

### Devoção & Poderes Concedidos

If the character follows a god, suggest the Divindade whose domains align with the character's values and story. Example: a character haunted by loss might follow Tenebra (darkness) or Thyatis (justice/light) — both are valid but tell very different stories.

### Perícias

Suggest skill proficiencies based on Origem + lived experience, not just class optimization.

### Character Concept Examples (Official)

Help the player describe their concept in one sentence that includes at least one personality trait:

- Uma bucaneira que sonha em conhecer os pontos mais distantes de Arton.
- Um guerreiro apaixonado pela nobre que só viu uma vez, determinado a se provar digno dela.
- Um mago atrapalhado e tímido, mas muito inteligente.
- Uma paladina que não consegue controlar seu pavio curto.
- Um anão clérigo de Valkaria — devotado à Deusa da Humanidade.
- Uma elfa amargurada e traumatizada com a queda do reino élfico.
- Uma guerreira cuja aldeia foi massacrada, que busca vingança e uma nova família.

## World Assumptions (Arton)

- **Tone**: High Fantasy + Anime/Manga influence. Over-the-top action is encouraged.
- **Key conflicts**: Purists vs Non-humans; Alliance vs Tormenta; Gods vs Each Other.
- **The Tormenta**: Always present. Characters either fight it, run from it, or carry it inside them.
- **Characters**: Often tragic backstories but heroic attitudes — the world demands both.
