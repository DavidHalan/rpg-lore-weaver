# Ordem Paranormal RPG Rules Summary (Backstory Focus)

## Key Concepts

### Setting: Modern-Day Brazil

- **Implication**: Urban horror set in contemporary Brazil. Characters are ordinary people recruited by a secret organization (Ordo Realitas) to investigate paranormal phenomena.
- **Tone**: Investigative horror with Brazilian cultural context. Think X-Files meets Lovecraft in São Paulo.

### O Outro Lado (The Other Side)

- **Core Concept**: A parallel dimension of pure paranormal energy. All supernatural events in OP originate from O Outro Lado bleeding into reality.
- **Backstory Impact**: How did the character first encounter O Outro Lado? Was it gradual (strange events) or sudden (a full breach)?

### NEX (Nível de Exposição / Exposure Level)

- **Mechanic**: Starts at 5% for new agents. Increases with exposure to the paranormal. Higher NEX = more power, but also more corruption.
- **Backstory**: NEX represents how much of O Outro Lado has seeped into the character. Even at 5%, something has already changed.
- **Key Question**: "What was the event that put you on the Ordo Realitas's radar?"

### Classes

- **Combatente**: Physical fighters, military or martial arts background
- **Especialista**: Investigators, hackers, social manipulators, technicians
- **Ocultista**: Researchers of the paranormal, ritual users, scholars of O Outro Lado

### Elementos (Paranormal Elements)

Each element represents a different aspect of O Outro Lado's influence:

- **Sangue (Blood)**: Life force manipulation, healing, biological horror
- **Morte (Death)**: Necromancy, entropy, connection to the dead
- **Energia (Energy)**: Raw power, electricity, kinetic force
- **Conhecimento (Knowledge)**: Divination, telepathy, forbidden truth
- **Medo (Fear)**: Terror manipulation, illusions, shadow control

> Ask: "Which element calls to your character, and what does that say about their inner nature?"

## Backstory Prompts

### Recruitment Questions

- How were you recruited by the Ordo Realitas? (Witnessed an event? Were a victim? Sought them out?)
- What was your life before the Ordo? (Normal job? Criminal past? Academic career? Military?)
- What did you have to leave behind when you joined? (Family? Career? Identity? Normalcy?)
- Who in your civilian life thinks you got a "government job in Brasília"?

### Paranormal Exposure

- What was your first encounter with O Outro Lado? Was it beautiful, terrifying, or both?
- Have you used a paranormal ability? How did it feel? Did it cost you something?
- Do you dream about O Outro Lado? What do you see?
- You've seen something that your training says is impossible. What was it?

### Personal Horror

- What is the one "normal" thing you cling to? (A routine, a relationship, a habit)
- When you look in the mirror after a mission, what do you notice that's different?
- What lie do you tell yourself to keep going?
- Who would you sacrifice the mission to save?

## Connections Prompts

- Who is your partner in the Ordo, and what's the one thing you don't tell them?
- Who outside the Ordo knows (or suspects) the truth about your job?
- What other agent do you trust with your life but not your secrets?
- Who from your past would be destroyed if they knew what you've seen?
- What handler or superior do you respect but fear?

## Mechanical Suggestions (Phase 6)

### Atributos

OP uses 5 attributes — derive from backstory:

- **Agilidade**: Physical reflexes, speed — from athletic or survival background
- **Força**: Raw power — from labor, combat, or physical training
- **Intelecto**: Education, analysis — from academic or research background
- **Presença**: Social influence, willpower — from leadership or performance background
- **Vigor**: Endurance, health — from hardship or physical resilience

### Class Selection

Choose based on the character's approach to the paranormal:

- **Combatente**: If the backstory emphasizes physical confrontation and protection
- **Especialista**: If the backstory emphasizes investigation, social skills, or technical expertise
- **Ocultista**: If the backstory emphasizes research, ritual, or direct contact with O Outro Lado

### Elemento Alignment

Suggest which Element fits the character's inner nature:

- A character driven by rage or protection → **Sangue**
- A character haunted by loss → **Morte**
- A character who craves power or control → **Energia**
- A character obsessed with truth → **Conhecimento**
- A character defined by their trauma → **Medo**

### Perícias (Skills)

Suggest based on pre-recruitment life:

- Ex-military: Luta, Pontaria, Tática, Atletismo
- Ex-academic: Investigação, Ciências, Medicina, Tecnologia
- Ex-criminal: Furtividade, Crime, Intimidação, Percepção
- Ex-journalist: Investigação, Diplomacia, Intuição, Profissão (jornalismo)

### Sanidade (Sanity)

Starting sanity is derived from Presença. The backstory should establish what "sane" looks like for this character, so the GM knows what breaking looks like.

## World Assumptions

- Modern-day Brazil with a hidden supernatural layer
- The Ordo Realitas is a secret organization with global reach
- O Outro Lado is incomprehensible and dangerous — every spell has a cost
- Characters are expendable; survival is not guaranteed
- Brazilian culture, geography, and social dynamics matter — set missions in real cities
- Horror is personal: the scariest thing is what O Outro Lado reveals about YOU
